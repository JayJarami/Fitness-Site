Meet PJ Stahl

WORLD CLASS TRAINING

PJ Stahl is a Los Angeles based Gym Owner and Personal Trainer who focuses on corrective exercise techniques to help clients recover and overcome chronic pain and imbalances. His clients include celebrities, professional athletes and everyday people. He utilizes over 20 years of experience, an extensive education in kinesiology, and numerous national certifications to provide innovating and safe training for his clients.
